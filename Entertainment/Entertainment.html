The Impact Of Technology On Entertainment

One key area in which computing has had a significant impact is the entertainment industry. Technology can be used in the entertainment industry for a diverse range of applications, for example distribution of media or tools allowing for new forms of entertainment to be created

The digital entertainment industry

Digital entertainment has become a huge global industry, with revenue made from digital entertainment estimated to exceed 1 trillion in 2025 [1]. Multiple Fortune 500 companies are either based on (e.g. netflix and disney) or have a significant presence in (e.g. alphabet with youtube, apple with appleTV). Digital entertainment refers to any form of entertainment that is primarily delivered through digital technology.

There are many different branches of the digital entertainment industry, including:
Steaming Video Games
One of the largest industries to come from the digital entertainment industry is the streaming industry. An industry that would have been entirely impossible without digital technology is the videogames industry.

Independant media

Another major change to entertainment made possible through improvements in technology is the rise of independant media

One way technology has made it much easier and cheaper for people with a limited budget and with a limited/single person team size to make many forms of media such as movies and videogames.

Another major way technology has enabled independant media is through making it far easier to distribute and advertise the technology.
