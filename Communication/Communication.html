Effect On Society

With the development of social media, people's vocabularies have changed and shortened with people using abbrieviations such as BRB ("Be Right Back") or GTG ("Got To Go").

This also extends to the use of emojis and reactions such as being able to 'thumbs up' or 'heart' messages on WhatsApp.

While whether these developements are positive or negative there is no doubt of their existence and effect on society.
